Privacy Policy

Ludo Mobile Application

Effective Date: April 26, 2026

At ABH Studios, we believe in complete transparency. This Privacy Policy outlines how our Ludo application handles user information.

1. Data Collection

The Ludo app is built to be a standalone, offline experience.

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, device IDs, or any tracking technologies to monitor your activity.

2. App Permissions

This application operates with minimal requirements. It does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, or location. All gameplay data (like current scores or active matches) is stored temporarily in your device's memory and is wiped when the app is closed or cleared.

3. Offline Functionality

Because this app does not require an internet connection to play, your data never leaves your device. There is no server-side storage and no data transmission to ABH Studios or any third party.

4. Children's Privacy

Our game is family-friendly and safe for children. Since we collect zero data, we are fully compliant with global privacy standards, including COPPA and GDPR.

5. Third-Party Links

This app does not contain advertisements or links to external third-party websites.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy or the app's functionality, please reach out to us.
