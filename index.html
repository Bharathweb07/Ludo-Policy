Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 26, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use the Ludo mobile application (the "App").

1. Information Collection and Use

The App is designed as a local, offline game. We prioritize your privacy and do not collect any user data.

  • Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Game Data: All gameplay progress and player names are stored locally on your device and are not shared with us or any third parties.
  • Location Data: The App does not track or access your physical location.

2. Permissions

The App does not require or request access to sensitive permissions such as your camera, microphone, contacts, or file storage.

3. Third-Party Services

This App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media SDKs that could track your behavior or collect data.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is safe for all ages. Because we do not collect any personal information, it is fully compliant with children's privacy regulations (such as COPPA).

5. Security

Since the App operates entirely offline and does not transmit data, your information is as secure as your physical device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be updated on this page with a new effective date.
